Toasty Delgit

Have you ever wanted to taste a mystery on your tongue? Read on!

Toasty delgit invokes chaos with each bite!

Ingredients

  • Sprouted grain bread
  • Suspicious liquid
  • Cereal bowl
  • 1/2 tsp celery salt
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt

Steps

  1. Toast the bread to the desired doneness
  2. Place the toasted toast in the cereal bowl
  3. Gently pour the suspicious liquid onto the toast, being careful to follow directions as specified by suspicious liquid manufacturer
  4. Add a pinch of each celery and kosher salts, reserving the rest
  5. Consume toasty delgit by sprinkling salt onto tongue before eating, as one does
