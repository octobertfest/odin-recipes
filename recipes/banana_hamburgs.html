Banana Hamburgs

Taste the taste of paradise with this unexpected combo!

You love bananas, you love hamburgs! Why haven't you thought of combining the two?

This dish was actually a delectable discovery born out of digging around in the kitchen trash for my keys one rainy April morning...

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Ground hamburg meat (40% lean/60% fat)
  • Bun
  • Pimento cheese

Steps

  1. Mash the banana
  2. Mix the banana and the ground hamburg meat, first incorporating the fat and then the lean portion
  3. Form mixture into patties and fry in frying pan until patty is dead and gone
  4. Smear pimento cheese on the bun
  5. Place patty onto bun and enjoy!
